Our approach towards the solution

01

Data Collection & Analysis:

Gather historical electricity usage data from appliances, households, or smart meters to understand consumption patterns.

02

AI Model Prediction

Use machine learning algorithms (like regression or neural networks) to estimate future electricity consumption based on the collected data

03

Validation & Optimization

Compare predicted usage with actual consumption to measure accuracy, then adjust the model for improved predictions and actionable energy-saving recommendations.

04

System Overload & Equipment Wear

Unoptimized electricity use puts unnecessary stress on appliances and wiring, leading to frequent breakdowns, reduced lifespan, and higher maintenance or replacement costs over time.
